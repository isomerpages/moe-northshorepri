# **Green Club**

The Green Club at Northshore Primary School fosters a passion for environmental service through our values of “eMpathy and Respect.” We aim to create awareness among our Green Club members about biodiversity, sustainability, and human impacts on the environment, cultivating knowledge (HEAD) and inspiring actions (HEART and HANDS).

Green Club members participate in a variety of hands-on activities, engaging in hydroponics projects that promote sustainable food production and responsible use of resources. Through their participation in eco-initiatives in school, students develop an understanding of sustainable practices and environmental stewardship.

The club strives to nurture and inspire environmentally conscious students who actively contribute to conservation and sustainability efforts, embodying empathy, respect, and a proactive commitment to environmental well-being within Northshore Primary School.
