# **Modern Dance**

The Modern Dance CCA provides students with opportunities to explore creative expression through movement and dance. Students develop coordination, confidence and teamwork as they demonstrate courage to explore new ideas and styles. As they prepare for the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF), students nurture a passion to learn through disciplined practice and perseverance, and develop a heart to serve by working together to represent the school with pride and commitment.
