# **Wushu**

Wushu provides a holistic blend of physical fitness, discipline, and cultural immersion for students. Under expert guidance, students develop flexibility, agility, strength and balance while mastering traditional martial arts techniques. From stances and forms to kicks and punches, students will learn foundational skills crucial for their full body awareness and coordination. In Wushu, students also learn to respect their peers, instructor, and the cultural context of this traditional Chinese martial art, fostering social awareness and teamwork. They are also instilled with essential values such as respect, perseverance, and self-control, nurturing them to be well-rounded individuals.
